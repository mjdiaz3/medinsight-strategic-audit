Strategic Findings

The "Handoff" Gap

While Buoy Health and Ada are excellent at identifying the issue, they fail at the handoff. Users are left with a diagnosis but no clear way to communicate it. We bridge this with the Clinician Brief.

Data vs. Privacy

K Health requires significant personal data sharing for its "matching" logic. MedInsight’s "Privacy First" focus for teens on parents' insurance is a powerful, untapped differentiator.

Education over Upselling

Giants like K Health prioritize funneling users into their own telehealth services. We remain an independent educator, providing cited sources to help users navigate their existing care.

Real-Time Market Pulse

Emerging Gaps

K Health is seeing a spike in user frustration regarding "scripted" interactions and technical disconnections. Ada is struggling with mental health and complex pre-existing conditions.

User Sentiment

Users are increasingly wary of "black box" AI. The demand for transparency and source-backed clarity (our core strength) is at an all-time high as safety organizations warn against AI hallucinations.

Strategic Opportunity

MedInsight should lean into human-in-the-loop preparation. By focusing on the "Clinician Brief," we address the primary user fear: that AI advice won't be taken seriously by real doctors.

Board Memo Talking Point

"We analyzed major competitors and identified a critical market gap: the transition from AI insight to professional care. While others focus on triage, MedInsight AI exploits this gap with the Clinician Brief—empowering users to speak confidently to their doctors. Our differentiation isn't just better AI; it's a better clinical outcome for the user."
